## Problems Solved

### Core Problems
1. **Scope Creep** - 40% of projects exceed original scope without billing
2. **Poor Estimates** - Estimates off by 30-50%, leading to missed deadlines
3. **Revenue Loss** - 15-25% revenue lost to unbilled hours and forgotten tasks
4. **Admin Overhead** - 20+ hours weekly spent on status updates vs. actual work
5. **Missed Deadlines** - 60% of projects delivered late due to poor planning
6. **Budget Overruns** - Projects exceed budget by 27% on average
7. **Client Churn** - Poor communication leads to 35% client dissatisfaction
8. **Team Burnout** - Unclear priorities cause 45% productivity loss

### How It Solves Them
- **AI-Powered Analysis**: Automatically detects scope creep by analyzing requirements vs. deliverables
- **Smart Risk Detection**: Identifies ambiguous requirements and potential scope expansion before it happens
- **Automated Invoicing**: Generates invoices based on tracked time and approved milestones
- **Real-time Analytics**: Visualizes team velocity, burn-down charts, and project health
- **Unified Workspace**: Combines Kanban, Gantt charts, and documentation in one interface
- **Stakeholder Questions**: AI generates critical questions to clarify requirements upfront

## Design Specifications

### Glassmorphism Implementation (Pure Glass, No Gradients)
- **Backdrop Blur**: 20-40px blur effect (`backdrop-filter: blur(20-40px)`)
- **Background**: Clean white overlay only (`rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.05-0.15)`)
  - Light glass: `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.05)`
  - Medium glass: `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.08)`
  - Heavy glass: `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.12)`
  - Strong glass: `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.15)`
- **Border**: 1px solid `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)` - minimal white border
- **Colored Accents**: Used ONLY for borders/icons, NOT as overlays
  - Accent borders: `rgba(128, 152, 249, 0.2-0.3)` for primary color
  - Accent icons: Full color `#8098F9` or `#d946ef`
- **Shadow**: Neutral soft shadow only (`rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.1-0.2)`)
- **NO Gradient Overlays**: Pure glass transparency is the aesthetic
- **Color Usage**: Colors appear as solid accents (borders, icons, text highlights), never as gradient overlays on glass

### Interactive Elements (Pure Glass Style)
- **Hover States**: Cards lift slightly (translateY -4px), border opacity increases (0.1 ‚Üí 0.2), NO glow effects
- **Buttons**: Clean glass buttons with solid color accents (NO gradients)
  - Primary: Glass background with solid primary color border `#8098F9`
  - Secondary: Glass background with solid secondary color border `#d946ef`
  - Hover: Border opacity increases, slight scale (1.02x)
- **Animations**: Subtle fade-ins, slide-ups, floating effects - all minimal and clean
- **Micro-interactions**: Smooth hover transitions, loading states with glass skeleton screens
- **Color Accents**: Used sparingly - borders, icons, text highlights only

### Responsive Design
- **Mobile**: Stack cards vertically, maintain glass effects
- **Tablet**: 2-column layouts where appropriate
- **Desktop**: Full 3-column layouts, enhanced animations

### Accessibility
- High contrast ratios for text
- Clear focus states
- Keyboard navigation support
- Screen reader friendly
