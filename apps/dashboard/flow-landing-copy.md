## FAQ SECTION

**Label:** FAQ

**H2:**
```
Questions before you join?
```

**Q1: How does scope detection work?**
You upload your project brief or SOW. Flow's AI reads it, generates a Scope Score (0–100), and identifies risks, ambiguities, and missing requirements — before work starts. During the project, you paste client requests and Flow checks them against the original brief in seconds.

**Q2: Do I need to change how my team works?**
No. Start by uploading a brief and seeing the Score. Most teams are up and running in under 10 minutes. The workflow fits around what you already do — it doesn't replace it.

**Q3: What if the client brief is in another language?**
Flow auto-detects and processes briefs in any language. Outputs are delivered in the language of your interface.

**Q4: Can I use this without a full team?**
Yes. The Freelancer plan is built for solo operators managing multiple clients. You get all the core scope protection — just without team features.

**Q5: What happens when scope creep is detected?**
Flow flags the request, shows you which part of the brief it conflicts with, estimates the cost of the extra work, and drafts a reply you can send to the client directly. You review and send — or edit and send.

**Q6: How is this different from ClickUp or Asana?**
Those tools track what's done. Flow protects what's owed. No project management tool reads your brief and tells you what's going to go wrong before work starts. That's the gap Flow closes.

**Q7: Is my data secure?**
Yes. All documents are encrypted in transit and at rest. We don't train our models on your project data.

**Q8: Can I get a demo?**
The live demo is on the homepage — upload any brief and see a real Scope Score in seconds. No signup required.
